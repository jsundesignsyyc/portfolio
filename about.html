About Me

J. Sun Designs

Located in Calgary, Alberta, J. Sun Designs is a student currently studying Interactive Design in Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT).

Always been fascinated by interactive designs, whether it be physical designs such as archetecture, or digital designs such as UI/UX.

I have always looked at different designs in life and thought: "How do I make this design better, use less effort and make this so that everyone can easily understand it?" Now I could just be lazy and don't want to go extra steps to acheive my goal, but I believe great design makes something accessible, understandable, and hassle-less to use to even the laziest of us.
